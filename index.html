Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need my own equipment?

No. Loaner bows, arrows, and safety gear are available for beginners. If you have your own equipment, bring it along.

Where are lessons held?

Lessons are held in Westerville, with indoor and outdoor options depending on the season. Exact location is shared upon booking.

What ages do you teach?

Generally ages 9 and up. For younger students, please reach out to discuss readiness and options.

What should I wear?

Close‑toed shoes and comfortable clothing. Avoid loose jewelry and overly baggy sleeves.
